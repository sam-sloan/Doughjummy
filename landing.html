    • ABOUT US

    DoughJummy is a young, talented, up and coming group from Hamilton, Ontario. With music that is inspired by their trials and tribulations, they are pushing the envelope. Despite their surroundings, they collectively define the phrase "Product Of My Environment" (P.O.M.E.)

    MUSIC

    The DoughJummy movement and story is being narrated by Fouryy and KLM. They have succeeded in developing a musical sound that is influenced by their surroundings in North America, and the U.K., infused with a style in Afro Beats that stems from their African upbringing.
